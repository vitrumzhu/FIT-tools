About the CdA calculator

Maybe the name CdA Calculator is a little bit misleading. Since one has to make quite a few assumptions under which this Calculator works maybe the name "CdA Estimator" would be more suitable - but who would Google this Keywords ;-).

To name the most important assumptions: flat course and no head or tailwind. Moreover it is difficult to get accurate numbers for the rolling resistance coefficient and the drivetrain efficiency.

Here you can find a Power to Speed Calculator together with some notes on the calculators and the formulas behind them.
